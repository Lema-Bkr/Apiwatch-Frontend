Becoming a precision beekeeper

We want to make your hive monitoring experience more intense. Mellisphera is a comprehensive interface that aggregates your hive data together with beekeeper actions and environmental weather. A set of unique algorithms provide actionable information. It’s no more about temperature and weight but about hive fitness, brood level, honey productivity, robing or swarming… Alerts and notifications! Read more

The good news is that Mellisphera is integrated with Broodminder. Once logged in you’ll inherit your full sensor history. Moreover this data will be enriched with daily averages, events, alerts etc. You’ll be able to explore past events and read the season development from a completely new perspective. In two words, you’re about to become a precision beekeeper.

Broodminder and Mellisphera - How it works

Mellisphera and Broodminder environments are complementary and closely linked. You’ll continue to use myBroodminder to set up your apiaries, hives and sensors. This information is updated into Mellisphera at your next login. Mellisphera will allow you to carry detailed analysis and explorations as well as sending you custom alerts and notifications. Tip : use your Broodminder account and password to directly log into Mellisphera
